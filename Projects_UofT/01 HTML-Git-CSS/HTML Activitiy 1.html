How to make a bacon egg & cheese

  1. Toast 2 slices of bread, any of your liking
  2. After toasting, place ya cheese on!
  3. Now, place cooked eggs on top. (Make sure these eggs seasoned with salt & pepper)
  4. Top it off with Bacon, make sure they crispy
  5. Close it off with other piece of toast
  6. Enjoy!
